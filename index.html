RememVR

RememVR is a Virtual Reality application, designed originally with people
with dementia in mind, for reminiscence therapy. It has since been expanded upon and is available for anyone.

The application is currently in a proof on concept phase.

If you would like to demo the application in its current state please contact me on : craig@rememvr.co.uk

About the app

The inital application was developed as a 4th year dissertation project at University, with the aim of enhancing and supporting digital reminiscence materials through the use of Virtual Reality (VR). The application was developed as a proof of concept prototype for reminiscence in healthy individuals who do not have dementia, with the aim to establish the feasibility and acceptability of using VR for people with dementia.
After thought on the application, the decision was made to present the application to a wider range of people and to not narrow it to people with dementia, allowing everyone to use the app to reminisce. The full dissertation can be found by clicking the button, which will download the .pdf file.

My Vision

Many people view Virtual Reality as a gimmick or just a gaming device, when really it is more than that. Virtual Reality is a powerful tool in not just gaming, but in fitness, healthcare and social spaces.
With more people unable to see loved ones due to the global Coronavirus Pandemic, now is the time to really use VR to it's full potential. Using VR to talk to and interact with friend and family without risking their safety for instance.
I aim to develop software and ideas to use Virtual Reality in the healthcare industry to help provide a greater quality of life for people.
