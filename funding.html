Business Plan

The app will be released to the public for FREE upon completion.
The aim for the application is to be accessible to anyone, so anyone can pick up a VR headset, launch the app and reminisce with friends, family and loved ones. Putting a price on the initial application would lower accessibility.

One of the aims is to offer the application to care homes and people in harder to reach communities. As technology can be quite confusing for a lot of people services will be offered to users at a price. These services are :
  • Purchase and setup of the Oculus Quest 2 VR Headset w/ Facebook account
  • Install and setup of the application
  • Tutorial and assistance in using the application
  • Training for staff/carers for effective use with elderly/people with dementia
  • Extra fee for custom 3dVR assets
  • Continued free support and helpline for use of the application
