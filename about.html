About the Application

The inital application was developed as a 4th year dissertation project at University, with the aim of enhancing and supporting digital reminiscence materials through the use of Virtual Reality (VR). The application was developed as a proof of concept prototype for reminiscence in healthy individuals who do not have dementia, with the aim to establish the feasibility and acceptability of using VR for people with dementia.
After thought on the application, the decision was made to present the application to a wider range of people and to not narrow it to people with dementia, allowing everyone to use the app to reminisce.
The application is a multiuser application originally designed for use by carers and people with dementia, allowing a carer to be in the same world as the person with dementia. As a result of this, the app can be used to connect with anyone with a VR headset around the world, to reminisce with friends, family or even strangers!
Using simple menu controls, the user would find their friends or create their own "house" and be transported into a relaxing house setting. The user would then use a remote control to change the media on the television in front of them, or even listen to some audio from the radio. Some objects would be available for the user to view and interact with.
